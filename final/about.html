Genre

The range of genres I listen to is really wide. For example: pyschedelic pop, rock, rap, AND what I listen to outside of this list can't all be accounted for. The artists on this website are chosen to show the display of sound I enjoy.

Meaning

Every single one of these musicians you're going to see have had an impact on me. Growing up each of them have helped me through rough spots, gotten me to understand things, and simply given me joy too. Some did these things more than others, but they're still memorable!
